IRC For Developers

IRC Still Works

Why are we paying for chat applications so our teams can communicate? Well, IRC has not always been secure, customizable or easy to deploy but a lot of these issues have now been soved. With a ZNC bounce, a simple ircd server and 5 minutes you can avoid paying for communication and keep your company chat logs secure and on your own infrastructure.

Komanda is open source, free and built using modern web technologies. Its very easy to extend, customize and secure. Try it out and stop paying for team communication tools.
